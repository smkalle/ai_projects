## Introduction

This tutorial will guide you through creating an **AI Headhunter Agent** using [LangGraph](https://docs.langchain.com/langgraph) and [Davia](https://docs.davia.dev), tools designed to automate LinkedIn recruitment.  

The goal is to make the hiring process faster and more efficient — especially for AI engineers looking to streamline candidate sourcing through automation and stateful agent design.

We’ll break it down into clear, practical steps so you can follow along and adapt the solution to your own workflows or domains.
