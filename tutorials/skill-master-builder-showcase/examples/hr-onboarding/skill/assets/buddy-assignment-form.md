## Buddy Selection Criteria (verify before assigning)

- [ ] Buddy is NOT the new hire's direct manager
- [ ] Buddy has 6+ months tenure at the company
- [ ] Buddy is a high performer and positive culture carrier
- [ ] Buddy has bandwidth for 30–60 min/day in Week 1, tapering to weekly by Day 30
- [ ] Buddy has been briefed on their role and responsibilities (see below)
- [ ] Buddy has agreed to this assignment

---

## Buddy Responsibilities

### Week 1 (Daily — ~30 min/day)
- [ ] Greet new hire on Day 1 and make initial introductions
- [ ] Answer "ambient" questions (where's the printer, how do I expense, etc.)
- [ ] Brief daily check-in: "How's it going? Anything confusing or unclear?"
- [ ] Introduce new hire to team members and informal networks
- [ ] Share cultural norms: meeting etiquette, communication style, team rituals

### Weeks 2–4 (2–3x per week — ~20 min each)
- [ ] Continue informal check-ins
- [ ] Help new hire navigate internal processes and politics
- [ ] Share tribal knowledge not in the employee handbook
- [ ] Report any disengagement signals to HR (not manager) immediately

### Days 30–90 (Weekly — ~15 min)
- [ ] Weekly brief check-in: "How are you feeling? Anything I can help with?"
- [ ] Continue introductions to broader organization as opportunities arise
- [ ] Provide honest feedback on culture integration to HR at 60-day mark

---

## What Buddy is NOT Responsible For

- Performance management or evaluation of the new hire
- Delivering formal training
- Compliance paperwork or HR processes
- Replacing the manager's 1:1 meetings
